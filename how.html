The Process

How Velour Works

Simple steps for drivers and clients alike

For Clients
1
Browse & Select
Explore our curated collection of premium vehicles. Filter by city, vehicle category, transmission, and daily rate. Every listing is uploaded directly by a verified driver.
2
View Details & Enquire
Click any listing to see the full vehicle details, driver profile, photos, and pricing. Use the enquiry button to reach out directly to the driver to confirm dates and availability.
3
Enjoy the Journey
Your verified driver arrives on time in an immaculate vehicle. Sit back, relax, and travel in distinction.
For Drivers
1
Register & Upload
Complete the 4-step registration form. Enter your personal details, your vehicle specifications, upload vehicle photos, and a professional portrait of yourself. Set your own daily rate.
2
Go Live Instantly
Once you submit your listing, it appears immediately on the Browse page for all clients to discover. No waiting period — your profile is live the moment you complete registration.
3
Earn & Grow
Accept booking enquiries directly via email. Set your availability, build your client reputation, and grow your earnings entirely on your own schedule.
"The finest journeys begin with a single registration."
